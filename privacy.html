Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

KBLock does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

  • All application data (rules, settings, statistics) is stored locally on your device.
  • iCloud Sync uses your personal iCloud account (NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore) — no external servers.
  • No analytics SDK, tracking code, or telemetry is included.
  • Accessibility access is used solely for switching keyboard input sources.
  • The built-in update checker (Sparkle) connects directly to GitHub Releases.
